# Enterprise BI Dashboards

A collection of Business Intelligence dashboards and reporting solutions delivered across Banking, Government, Education, and Private sectors.

## Overview

These dashboards were designed to support operational, tactical, and executive decision-making through scalable reporting, KPI monitoring, and self-service analytics.

The portfolio includes examples inspired by enterprise reporting projects involving:

* Banking & Financial Services
* Government Organizations
* Education & Scholarship Programs
* Regulatory Reporting
* Operational Performance Monitoring

## Key Capabilities

### Executive Dashboards

* KPI scorecards
* Trend analysis
* Performance monitoring
* Executive reporting

### Operational Dashboards

* SLA tracking
* Request management
* Workload monitoring
* Process performance analytics

### Financial & Business Dashboards

* Revenue reporting
* Budget tracking
* Forecasting support
* Management reporting
